AHOBILAM

Situated 350 km from Hyderabad, Ahobilam is a significant holy place in the Kurnool district of Andhra Pradesh. It is one of the in best trekking places in Hyderabad where people reach by trekking through thick forest and rocky paths. Here, Lord Narasimha is worshipped in 9 different forms for which separate temples are also constructed.
Best time to visit: October to March
Visit duration: 1-2 days

KONDAMADUGU

Situated 40 km from Hyderabad, this is one of the best places surrounded by villages and hills where you can experience different adventures activities. The peaceful and lush green spaces will fill you with lots of enthusiasm to reach the top of the hill. It is the perfect weekend gateway for many local people of Hyderabad.
Best time to visit: October to March
Visit duration: Within 1 day

MEDAK FORT

It is one of the most visited places by the tourist and also by trekking lovers. The fort has 500 steps to reach the top and therefore, reaching to the fort is itself trekking. The fort covers 100 acres of land where you will confront many challenges to reach different areas of the fort.
Best time to visit: During monsoon
Visit duration: 1-2 days

ANANTAGIRI HILLS

Popular for trekking camps in Hyderabad, it is also a famous destination in Telangana for tourist. The two trekking trials Anantha Padmanabha Swamy temple and towards Kereilli in the dense forests will add experience in your life. Enjoy the viewpoint on the top of the hill and beautiful freshwater streams while trekking.
Best time to visit: October to March
Visit duration: Within a day

KANAKAI WATERFALL

It is one of the favorable trekking spots near Hyderabad for all the trekkers. The Kanakai waterfall, Bandrev waterfall and Cheekati Gundam are located near to each other which you can visit at a time. It will take around 3-4 hours to explore all the streams.
Best time to visit: August to October
Visit duration: 4-5 km walks to reach the waterfall
